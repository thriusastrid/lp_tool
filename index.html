About This Seminar

Built to Promote
Financial Discipline

This initiative combines academic rigor with practical tools to help Filipino students manage their resources through mathematical optimization.

📊

Analyze Spending Behavior

Identify patterns in how students allocate their weekly allowance across essential and non-essential categories.

🎯

Optimize Allocation

Apply Linear Programming to find the mathematically optimal distribution that satisfies all minimum constraints.

🧠

Promote Financial Literacy

Equip students with both conceptual understanding and actionable budgeting strategies for real-world application.

⚖️

Balance Needs vs Wants

Distinguish between essential expenses and discretionary spending to prevent financial shortfalls mid-semester.

🔢

Apply Quantitative Methods

Demonstrate how Simplex algorithm-based LP solutions can be applied to everyday personal finance problems.

🌱

Build Saving Habits

Encourage consistent saving by treating savings as an objective function to be maximized within the LP framework.
